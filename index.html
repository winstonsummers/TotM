Commands

Here is a list of commands that Totm knows. You can also type something in to see it appear in the log book.

  • "PICK-UP" add an item to your inventory from the room
  • "NEXT-ROOM" moves you to the next room
  • "PREVIOUS-ROOM" moves you to the previous room(totm only remebers one room back, further rooms back will be empty)
  • "TALK-TO" talk to a person or monster in the room
  • "CAST-SPELL" cast a memorized spell from your spell or prayer book
  • "DETECT-TRAPS" search for and disarm traps
  • "LISTEN-AT-DOOR" listen at the door to get an idea of what is in the next room
  • "REST" rest for the night to recover hp and memorize spells
  • "SELL" sell item from inventory
  • "BUY" buy item from in the room
  • "LOAD" load a previously made character
  • "EQUIP" equip and item from inventory
  • "UNEQUIP" read equip and take a guess :P
  • "LEVEL-UP" increase the level of your character
  • "RESTART" start at the begining with nothing and no one
